UK Fetish Alliance

The UK Fetish Alliance exists to promote the LGBT Fetish community and lifestyle in the UK

Through social activisim, community outreach and engagement, we aim to promote and educate on healthy, consensual and fun Fetish lifestyles. Along with our allies, both in the hetero-sexual and LGBT communities, we will show and foster support for issues affecting the LGBT Fetish community. Here are some example areas we want to promote:

  • Fetish community participation in PRIDE marches around the UK
  • Transgender community support
  • Counter traditional demonisation of the Fetish community

Our group will be increasingly active over the next few months as we formalise our mission statement and objectives
